About Me

Howdy! My name is Jason Lin (林傑森). I graduated from Texas A&M University with a PhD in Computer Engineering.
I joined the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the National Chung Hsing University in 2021 as a faculty member.
Before that, I was with the International School of Technology and Management at Feng Chia University.

For Prospective Students

We are actively looking for self-motivated students to join our team at the Advanced Computing Laboratory.
People with basic experience in the areas of Information Security and Artificial Intelligence will be preferred.
You are more than welcome to pay a visit to our lab at Room 922B in the Science Building of the Main Campus.

Research Interests

My research mainly focuses on the fundamental issues of Text Mining and the use of Process Mining techniques to explore the coreference patterns of textual descriptions and mathematical expressions from scientific documents.
Before diving into this field, I was actively involved in a project sponsored by the ONR, which developed a situational awareness system based on the geospatial analysis of historical conflict events to model the insurgency behavior of asymmetric warfare.
Apart from the above research, I have also studied Quantum Computing and published several significant results in prestigious journals.
Please feel free to shoot me an email if you need any further information.
