About Me

Howdy! My name is Jason Lin (林傑森). I graduated from Texas A&M University with a PhD in Computer Engineering.
I joined the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the National Chung Hsing University as a tenured-track faculty member in February 2021.

Research Interests

My current research in AI mainly focuses on the following three tracks: (1) The development of Text Mining software to deal with the fundamental issues of scientific document understanding;
(2) The study of Process Mining techniques to address security and efficiency issues in management information systems; (3) The applications of machine learning to solve agricultural problems in practice.
Apart from the above research, I have also studied security issues in Quantum Computing and published several significant results in prestigious journals.
Please feel free to shoot me an email if you need any further information.
