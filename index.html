Welcome to ISLab!

The ISLab belongs to the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the National Chung Hsing University in Taiwan. We are actively engaged in computer security research, focusing on developing computational solutions to address real-world security problems. Our technical strengths empower us to advance knowledge and create meaningful impact through interdisciplinary collaboration. Some research topics being actively pursued include:

  • Lightweight Quantum Communication
  • Multimedia Security and Applications
  • Artificial Intelligence in Cybersecurity
  • Document Reverse Engineering
  • Engineering Education and Teaching Methods
