The Advanced Computing Laboratory (先進計算實驗室) belongs to the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the National Chung Hsing University (國立中興大學). In addition to our research in solving various advanced computing problems, we are extensively engaged in interdisciplinary research. Computational solutions for complex problems give us technology strengths, and the interdisciplinary research can leverage these strengths to improve the world. Some research topics being actively pursued include:

  • Content Analysis of Digital Documents
  • Engineering Education and Teaching Methods
  • Situational Awareness System Design
  • Semi-quantum Communications
  • Multimedia Authentications
