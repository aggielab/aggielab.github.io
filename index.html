The Advanced Computing Lab belongs to the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the National Chung Hsing University. In addition to our research in solving various advanced security problems, we are extensively engaged in interdisciplinary research. Computational solutions for complex problems give us technical strengths, and the interdisciplinary research can leverage these strengths to improve the world. Some research topics being actively pursued include:

  • Semi-quantum Communications
  • Multimedia Authentication
  • Anomaly Detection for Chiller System
  • Artificial Intelligence in Agriculture
  • Content Analysis of Digital Documents
  • Engineering Education and Teaching Methods
