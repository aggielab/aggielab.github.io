Welcome to ISLab!

The Information Security Laboratory (ISLab), established in February 2021, belongs to the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the National Chung Hsing University in Taichung City. We are actively involved in interdisciplinary research, aiming to provide computational solutions for complex problems. Our technical strengths empower us to make advancements that contribute to improving the world through interdisciplinary collaboration. Some research topics being actively pursued include:

  • Lightweight Quantum Communication
  • Multimedia Security and Applications
  • Artificial Intelligence in Cybersecurity
  • Document Reverse Engineering
  • Engineering Education and Teaching Methods
