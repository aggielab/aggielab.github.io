The AC lab belongs to the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at National Chung Hsing University. In addition to our research in solving various advanced computing problems, we are extensively engaged in interdisciplinary research. Computing solutions for complex problems give us technology strengths, and the interdisciplinary research can leverage these strengths to improve the world. Some research topics being actively pursued include:

  • Content Analysis of Digital Documents
  • Engineering Education and Teaching Methods
  • Situational Awareness System Design
  • Semi-quantum Communications
  • Multimedia Authentications
