About Me

Howdy! My name is Jason Lin (林傑森). I graduated from Texas A&M University with a PhD in Computer Engineering.
I have joined the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the National Chung Hsing University as a faculty member since February 1, 2021.
Please feel free to shoot me an email if you need any further information.

Research Interests

My current research mainly focuses on various advanced security issues in Quantum Computing.
So far, I have published several significant results in prestigious journals and international conferences.
Besides, I have also worked on some real-life problems in cross disciplines that require AI solutions including but not limited to:
(1) The development of Text Mining tools to deal with scientific document understanding;
(2) The applications of machine learning to address agricultural problems in practice;
(3) The study of Process Mining techniques to discover workflow models in IT systems.
