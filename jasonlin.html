About Me

Howdy! My name is Jason Lin. I graduated from Texas A&M University with a PhD in Computer Engineering.
I am currently a faculty member in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at National Chung Hsing University.
Please feel free to contact me if you need any further information.

Research Interests

My current research mainly focuses on the design of security protocols in Quantum Communication.
So far, I have published several significant results in prestigious journals and international conferences.
In addition to the aforementioned research, I have also worked on real-life cross-disciplinary problems, including:
(1) The study of Business Process Mining algorithms to discover workflow models in IT systems;
(2) The application of data analysis techniques to address practical problems in the agricultural field;
(3) The development of Text Mining tools for the automatic understanding of scientific documents.
