About Me

Howdy! My name is Jason Lin (LinkedIn) (Google Scholar). I am currently a PhD candidate under the supervision of Professor Jyh-Charn Liu in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Texas A&M University, College Station, TX.

Research Interests

My research mainly focuses on the fundamental issues of Document Engineering and the use of Business Process Mining techniques to extract the relational properties of textual descriptions and mathematical objects from STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) publications. Before diving into this field, I was actively involved in ONR-sponsored research that developed a situational awareness system based on Geospatial Intelligence and conflict events to model the tactical behavior of asymmetric warfare. Besides, I also have done a lot of research in Quantum Cryptography and published several papers in prestigious journals. Please feel free to contact me if you need any further information.
